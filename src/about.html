About EOHM

Electronic Organization for Humanitarian Media

We are an independent non-profit organization dedicated to combating violence, discrimination, and hate speech through innovative media content and digital projects that promote diversity and peace.

  • Our Mission

    To build a culture of peace through innovative media content and digital projects that combat violence, discrimination, and hate speech while promoting diversity, human rights, and social justice.

  • Our Vision

    A world where media serves as a bridge for understanding, where diverse voices are heard and respected, and where digital platforms become tools for peace-building and social cohesion.

  • Our Values

    Integrity, inclusivity, innovation, and impact. We believe in the power of authentic storytelling, diverse perspectives, cutting-edge technology, and measurable positive change in communities.

    Our Story

    Founded in 2020 during a time of unprecedented global challenges, EOHM emerged from a recognition that media and digital platforms could be powerful forces for positive change. Our founders, a group of journalists, activists, and technologists from Yemen and beyond, came together with a shared vision of using media to build bridges rather than walls.

    In a region often portrayed through the lens of conflict, we saw an opportunity to tell different stories â€“ stories of resilience, hope, cultural richness, and human dignity. We began by documenting the experiences of ordinary people facing extraordinary circumstances, giving voice to those who are often unheard in mainstream media narratives.

    Over the years, our work has expanded to include investigative journalism, cultural preservation projects, peace-building initiatives, and digital literacy programs. We have partnered with local communities, international organizations, and fellow media outlets to amplify important stories and create meaningful change.

    Our Approach

    We believe in a holistic approach to humanitarian media that combines rigorous journalism, community engagement, and innovative technology.

    01

    Community-Centered

    We work directly with communities to ensure their voices are heard and their stories are told with authenticity and respect.

    02

    Evidence-Based

    Our reporting and advocacy are grounded in thorough research, fact-checking, and verification processes.

    03

    Collaborative

    We partner with local organizations, international NGOs, and fellow media outlets to amplify impact and reach.

    04

    Innovative
